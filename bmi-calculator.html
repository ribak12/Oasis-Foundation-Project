Wellness notes

How to read the result

Keep the explanation simple for patients visiting the site for the first time. This makes the page useful without feeling too clinical.

Use it as a guide

BMI is a quick screening tool. It is useful for general awareness, but it does not fully measure muscle mass, body fat distribution, or medical history.

Hydration matters

The water estimate helps patients remember a simple daily target. Hot weather, exercise, and illness can increase water needs.

Know when to ask

If the result is far below or above the healthy range, the page gently encourages users to book a consultation for better advice.
